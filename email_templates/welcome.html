Security

To make your schedule accessible to all your devices and programs, the calendar must be: 1) publically available; and 2) hosted on my own personal server.

  1. This means that anyone with your schedule link could view your calendar. To minimize the risk of this, the calendar file does not actually use your name, but rather a code name. These names are generally a long enough/complex enough that it makes someone guessing your calendar very unlikely.
  2. While my own servers are also secured, I would suspect the safety of the AHS Network would still be superior. To my knowledge all major steps have been taken to secure the server, but this can change rapidly and it can take time to implement fixes.

Disclaimers

This program relies on a number of assumptions to properly generate a correct work schedule. There are numerous factors that can cause issues with the generation of these schedules, such as new shift codes, typos in the Microsoft Excel document, or duplicate names on the spreadsheet. While I do try to account for many of these, it will never be perfect. To reduce these risks, you will be emailed whenever your updated schedule varies from the previous version of the schedule. You will also receive a list of all the assumptions the program made. You are strongly encouraged to review these emails for any possible errors.

There is also the risk that there will be an issue with my own servers that may cause delays in schedule updates. While I do try to limit these occurrences, there is always the possibility of unexpected issues that can take several days (or longer) to resolve.

Finally, this whole project is done on my own spare time with my own resources. I am happy to provide some degree of assistance in using these programs (largely in the forms of tutorials or how-to documents), but from a practical standpoint I cannot provide individual and personalized tech support. I also cannot dedicate large portions of the work day to this, as it does not fall within the scope of my AHS employment.

Customizability

You will have the option of customizing various aspects of your calendar, including:

  • Choosing your anonymous calendar name
  • Having the calendar generate shifts as "Full Day" events or as specific events with start and stop times
  • Customize start and stop times for specific shifts
  • Set a reminder of upcoming shifts ranging from 0 to 60 minutes in advance

Activating Your Account

Should you wish to make use of any of the calendar customization options or to see details on to sync your schedule to your calendar program, you will need to activate your account with studybuffalo.com. You will be able to use the email associated with your calendar to login. Your will first need to set a password by visiting https://www.studybuffalo.com/accounts/password/reset/ and entering the email for your account. Follow the details in the received email to set a password for your account (please check your spam folder if you do not receive it right away). Once your account is activated, you may visit https://www.studybuffalo.com/rdrhc-calendar/ to make changes to your calendar, view instructions on syncing your schedule, and view all the above disclaimers again.
